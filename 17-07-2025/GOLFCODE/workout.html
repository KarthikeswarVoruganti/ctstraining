Workout Tips

Train smarter, recover faster, and push your limits — the right way

🔁 Warm-Up & Cool-Down

Start every session with 5–10 minutes of dynamic stretches, and end with static holds to boost mobility and reduce injury risk.

🏋️ Progressive Overload

Gradually increase resistance, reps, or sets over time. This helps build strength and keeps your body adapting.

⏱️ Rest Days Matter

Your muscles grow during rest — not while training! Schedule active recovery days like yoga or walking.

🧠 Mind-Muscle Connection

Focus on feeling the target muscle during each rep. Better activation leads to better results.
